About Us

Kenya Volleyball Federation

The Kenya Volleyball Federation is in charge of the volleyball activities within the coutry, Kenya.The Federation moderates all matches in the national league and also facilitates coaching and refereeing courses nationwide.

The Federation also collaborates with the African and internatuinal societies to ensure the national league is up to par with current standards and championships.
