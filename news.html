Malaba Tournament

The General Service Unit (GSU) team are the just concluded Malaba tournament winners. The team conquered a five set thriller against the Kenya Defence Forces (KDF) team in the semis to play Kenya Ports Authority (KPA) in the finals.

The winners walked away with a whooping one hundred thousand shillings from the tournament which was sponsored by Hon. Brian Odera, the area member of parliament.

The match between Trail blazers and Kenya Prisons did not end due to the ongoing heavy rains

The Ladies' side was dominated by teh Kenya pipeline team who thrashed the Kenya prisons team three sets to one and went home witha similar amount, one hundred thousand shillings
